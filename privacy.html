Privacy Policy for GO TEAM CARS Fleet Planner

This Privacy Policy explains what the app does, what data is stored in the app, and how that data is handled.

Last updated: March 12, 2026 Offline Android app No user accounts

Summary

  • GO TEAM CARS Fleet Planner is an offline vehicle catalog and planning application.
  • User data remains stored locally on the same device.
  • The app does not offer booking, payments, accounts, or live rental services.
  • The current Android build does not request dangerous permissions.

1. What the app does

GO TEAM CARS Fleet Planner allows users to:

  • View the local vehicle catalog included in the app.
  • Search, sort, and filter vehicles.
  • Save favorite vehicles on the device.
  • Select vehicles for comparison on the device.
  • Write notes on the device.
  • Save local vehicle estimates on the device.

The app does not place bookings or reservations, process payments, create user accounts, or provide live online rental services.

2. Data handling

Based on the current Android build, the app does not collect data from the device for transmission to GO TEAM CARS or to third parties.

The app stores only limited local data created by the user inside the app on the same device, including:

Favorite vehicles Comparison selections User notes Latest saved estimate

This data remains in the app's local storage on the device and is not transmitted from the app to GO TEAM CARS or to third parties.

3. Personal information

The app does not automatically collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, payment information, messages, or government identifiers.

However, the app includes an optional notes field. If a user chooses to enter personal information into that notes field, that content is stored locally on the device only and is not transmitted by the app.

4. Permissions

Based on the current Android manifest, the app does not request dangerous permissions.

5. Internet and third-party services

Based on the current Android build, the app does not require internet access for normal use.

The app does not include:

  • Account registration
  • Cloud sync
  • Remote databases
  • Analytics SDKs
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Payment processing
  • Booking submission
  • User tracking features

6. Data sharing

The app does not share user data with GO TEAM CARS, advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties from the current Android build.

7. Data retention and deletion

Locally stored favorites, comparison selections, notes, and estimates remain on the device until the user removes them, clears app storage, or uninstalls the app.

If the user clears app storage or uninstalls the app, locally stored data may be removed from the device.

8. Children

This app is a general vehicle catalog and planning app. It is not specifically directed to children.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's functionality or data handling changes. The updated version should be published with a new effective date.
